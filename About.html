HOW IT ALL STARTED

Dear Readers, We shall try to keep it short and we really hope that our story inspires you to expand your boundaries and persuade you to follow your ambitions. These are trying times, amidst this pandemic it all feels chaotic, it all is draining us down. Entrapped in our 9-5 jobs, in a 2000 sq.-ft home, people have forgotten to live for themselves.

We too suffered and we suffered bad, one of our team members just moved from his home town. He was really excited to start his new college life. Unfortunately his happiness was short-lived, as every personâ€™s life got restricted to a 15-inch screen amid the lockdown. He knew no one in the city, so depression and anxiety caught him. Due to the lack of companionship he literally had zero zeal to run after his passion of Cricket.

One day he was coming home after running some errands and saw us playing cricket. When we saw him standing alone and watching us play, we invited him to join us. Sometimes, all we need is a companion, a companion that we share commonalities with, a companion with whom we can pursue that long lost hobby and this inspired the idea of hobcom.
